About Our Service

We facilitate performance management, learning and development, and ensure compliance with labor laws. Key services include self-service portals, applicant tracking, and robust reporting. Universities benefit from increased efficiency, data accuracy, and improved employee experience. The HRMS manages faculty-specific needs like tenure tracking and student worker management, while also handling grant funding allocations and union agreement compliance. Ultimately, it enables data-driven decision-making for strategic workforce planning.

Job Vacancies

Software Developer

We are looking for a skilled Software Developer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have experience with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and backend technologies. Responsibilities include developing and maintaining web applications, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and ensuring the performance and scalability of our systems.

Project Manager

We are seeking an experienced Project Manager to oversee our projects from inception to completion. The successful candidate will have a proven track record in project management, excellent communication skills, and the ability to lead and motivate teams. Responsibilities include planning, executing, and closing projects, managing resources, and ensuring project goals are met.

HR Specialist

We are looking for an HR Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have experience in recruitment, employee relations, and HR compliance. Responsibilities include managing the recruitment process, conducting interviews, and ensuring compliance with labor laws and company policies.
